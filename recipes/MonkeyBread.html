Monkey Bread

Description

Monkey Bread: A scrumptious, gooey treat that's like edible bubble wrap—each pull-apart piece is as satisfying as popping a sweet little parcel of joy!

Ingredients

  • 3 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 2 1/4 tsp active dry yeast
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 1/3 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Mix dry ingredients, add wet ingredients, knead dough.
  2. Shape dough into balls.
  3. Dip balls in melted butter, then coat with cinnamon-sugar.
  4. Layer balls in greased pan, let rise.
  5. Bake at 350°F (175°C) for 25-30 mins.
  6. Cool, then enjoy pulling apart the tasty Monkey Bread!
