The Garbage Plate

Description

It's like a food party on a plate! Imagine a crazy mix of fries, meat, and salad, all hanging out together, breaking all the food rules and having a blast!

Ingredients

  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 cup water
  • 12 ounces tomato paste
  • 1⁄2 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 3⁄4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1 1⁄2 teaspoons paprika
  • 1⁄2teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1⁄2 teaspoon allspice
  • 1⁄4 teaspoon cinnamon
  • 1⁄2 teaspoon powdered clove

Steps

  1. In large skillet fry chopped onion and garlic in oil until soft. Add meat, stirring constantly with fork to keep its texture fine.
  2. Once meat is cooked add water and tomato paste, cook for 10 minutes.
  3. Then add sugar and spices. Cook 30 minutes, adding water if needed.
