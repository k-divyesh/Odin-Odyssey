Frog In The Pond

Description

Frog in the Pond: It's wobbly green jelly with a chocolate frog taking a dip, like a sweet surprise in a tadpole's swimming pool!

Ingredients

  • Green gelatin (jelly)
  • Chocolate frogs (small chocolate candies shaped like frogs)
  • Water

Steps

  1. Make green gelatin following packet instructions. Pour a bit into serving cups or a dish, filling about one-third.
  2. Refrigerate gelatin until partially set (1-2 hours).
  3. Place chocolate frogs on the partially set gelatin.
  4. Pour the remaining green gelatin over the frogs to cover them completely.
  5. Chill until fully set (several hours or overnight).
  6. Serve and enjoy your playful "Frog in the Pond" dessert!
